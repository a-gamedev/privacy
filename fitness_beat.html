Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 9, 2026

AGAMDEV ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Fitness Beat mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The App stores the following data locally on your device using SQLite database:

  • Equipment Information: Names and categories of fitness equipment you add
  • Breathing Patterns: Custom breathing configurations (inhale/exhale/breath-hold durations, repetition counts)
  • Training History: Records of your completed training sessions including duration, equipment used, and breathing patterns
  • App Settings: Your preferences for language, voice settings, and other app configurations

1.2 Data That Is NOT Collected

We do not collect:

  • Personal identifying information (name, email, phone number)
  • Location data
  • Health or fitness data from third-party services
  • Analytics or usage statistics
  • Any data transmitted to external servers

2. How We Use Your Information

All data stored by the App remains on your device and is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain the App's core functionality
  • Save your equipment list and breathing patterns
  • Track your training history
  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Manage your purchase status (Pro version unlock)

3. In-App Purchases

The App offers an optional one-time purchase to unlock the Pro version:

  • Free Version: Limited to 2 training items, 5 training sessions per item, 2 sets per session, and 5 reps per set
  • Pro Version: Unlimited training items, sessions, sets (up to 10), and reps (up to 50)

Purchase status is stored locally on your device. No payment information is collected or stored by us. All transactions are handled by Google Play Store.

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not share your data with any third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device, no information is transmitted to us or any external servers.

5. Data Security

We value your trust and strive to use commercially acceptable means of protecting your information. However, please remember that no method of electronic storage or transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. Since we do not collect any personal information, the App is safe for users of all ages.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
